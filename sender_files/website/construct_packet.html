Hello! Welcome to the packet configuration page, where you'll be able to craft the packet of your dreams. First things, first:
  • You gotta start selecting which layers you want in this packet. Just click the add layer button, and select which layer you want.
  • Next, you gotta configure what's inside each layer. Click the layer you want to edit, and a bunch of fields will pop up.
  • And then you just fill out those fields, and click the dandy 'Done' button!
"But wait," I hear you say, "doesn't an Ethernet frame have a checksum / IP packet have a type field / UDP have a length field / etc." YES! However, we here at PPP believe that we are in the future. So we'll fill those out automatically for you.
